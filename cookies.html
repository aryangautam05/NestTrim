Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how NestTrim uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. We are committed to transparency and empowering you with clear information about how your data is used.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, recognize you on return visits, and deliver a better experience.

Cookies can be "session cookies" that expire when you close your browser, or "persistent cookies" that remain for a set period or until you delete them.

We also use similar technologies like web beacons and tracking pixels to collect usage data to improve our services.

This policy informs you about the types of cookies we use, your choices, and how to manage cookies on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several categories of cookies, each serving a specific purpose:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for website operation and security. They enable core functionality like page navigation and secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data on website usage like page visits and load times to improve performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences such as language, location, or login settings to personalize your experience.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and marketing content based on your browsing activity.
